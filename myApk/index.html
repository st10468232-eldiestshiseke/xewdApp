Our Mission

Established in 2022, Empowering the Nation offers comprehensive training courses in Johannesburg. We've empowered hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners through our specialized programs.

Our founder, Precious Radebe, created this initiative to support community members who were never given the chance to upskill themselves or pursue formal qualifications. We believe in providing practical skills that make our students more marketable and confident in their careers.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every domestic worker and gardener in Johannesburg has access to quality education and practical skills training.

Our goal is to create sustainable livelihoods and foster a community of confident, skilled professionals.

6-Month Course

A full program designed for learners who want in-depth training and professional preparation. Covers advanced techniques, hands-on practice, and includes certification. Perfect for those aiming for long-term career opportunities.

6-Week Course

A short, focused training program to quickly gain essential skills. Ideal for learners who want to enter the workforce fast and build strong foundational knowledge.

Domestic Work Skills

Practical training in household management, cleaning, laundry, and organization. Designed to improve employability and efficiency in domestic work roles.

Gardening & Landscaping

Learn gardening techniques, landscaping basics, and outdoor maintenance. Perfect for those wanting to work in garden care or landscaping services.

Child Care

Training focused on child safety, nutrition, play-based learning, and first aid. Ideal for those pursuing work as nannies or daycare assistants.

Cooking & Nutrition

Covers food preparation, kitchen safety, meal planning, and nutrition basics. Perfect for learners interested in cooking professionally or in households.
