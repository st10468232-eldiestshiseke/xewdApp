Courses We Offer

Explore our wide range of skills training programs designed to empower and uplift individuals across South Africa.

Child Minding

Learn the essentials of caring for children, including nutrition, hygiene, and early development. This course prepares learners to work as professional childminders or nannies.

  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Certificate included
  • Hands-on practical training

Cooking

Learn to prepare healthy, delicious meals with a focus on hygiene, presentation, and nutrition. Ideal for aspiring chefs and domestic cooks.

  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Includes food safety module
  • Recipe book provided

Garden Maintenance

Master lawn care, planting, watering systems, and landscaping skills to maintain and beautify gardens efficiently.

  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Practical sessions included
  • Includes basic landscaping training

First Aid

Learn emergency care, CPR, and how to handle injuries and burns. Perfect for workplaces, households, and caregivers.

  • Duration: 3 Weeks
  • Accredited certification
  • Practical simulations included

Sewing & Crafts

Learn sewing techniques, repairs, and creative crafting for home or business. Gain hands-on experience with different fabrics and tools.

  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Materials provided
  • Project-based learning
